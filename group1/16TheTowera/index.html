16. The Tower
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Sudden Disruption
The Tower reflects unexpected events that shake your sense of stability. Losing your job is one such event, and this card acknowledges the intensity of that experience.
Breaking Down Illusions
This card suggests that the job loss may have revealed truths you were previously unaware of. For example, the role or workplace might not have been as secure or fulfilling as you believed.
Clearing the Way
The Tower's destruction isn’t without purpose. It tears down what no longer serves you, making space for something more aligned with your true self and aspirations.
Embracing Change
While The Tower’s changes can feel forced or sudden, they often lead to breakthroughs. This upheaval can be the catalyst for reevaluating your goals, passions, and direction.
Resilience and Rebuilding
This card urges you to focus on resilience. Though the fall may be painful, the opportunity to rebuild is profound and holds the potential for growth and renewal.

Suggestions and Actions

Acknowledge Your Feelings
Allow yourself to process the emotions that come with sudden change—whether it’s fear, grief, or anger. Suppressing these feelings will only delay healing.
Reflect on What’s Been Revealed
Consider what this upheaval has taught you. Did the job loss highlight an imbalance in your life? Were there aspects of your role or workplace that no longer aligned with your values?
Accept and Adapt
The Tower calls for acceptance of the situation as it is. Fighting the change will only cause more resistance. Instead, focus your energy on adapting and finding new paths forward.
Identify Opportunities Amid Chaos
As destabilizing as The Tower feels, it creates space for new opportunities. Think about what you’ve always wanted to do but haven’t pursued. This could be a chance to follow a dream or explore a completely new field.
Rebuild with Intention
Use this disruption as a chance to start fresh. Reevaluate what you want in a job or career and begin to build toward it with clarity and purpose. Consider seeking guidance from a career coach or mentor to help you structure your goals.

Final Thought

The Tower upright is a card of profound change and transformation. While the process can feel overwhelming, it is ultimately necessary for growth and renewal. Trust that by embracing this upheaval and focusing on rebuilding with intention, you’ll create a stronger and more fulfilling future. From the rubble of the old, something new and extraordinary can emerge.
