20. Judgment
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Self-Reflection and Evaluation
Judgment urges you to reflect on your past experiences, especially your job and career path. This might be a time to assess what worked and what didn’t in your previous role, to better understand what you truly want moving forward. It’s about acknowledging your strengths, acknowledging past mistakes or lessons, and using that insight to create a new path.
A Time for Rebirth
Losing your job might feel like an ending, but Judgment suggests this is actually an opportunity for rebirth. It’s a time to transform your career or life direction and emerge renewed. This card encourages you to embrace change and view this moment as an opening to start fresh.
Call to Action
The Judgment card indicates that now is the time to make important decisions that will affect your future. You may be called to take bold action or make changes to how you approach your career. This is not a time for passivity—Judgment urges you to step into your power and take responsibility for the direction you want to go in.
Awakening to Your True Purpose
Judgment is often linked to spiritual awakening and realizing your higher purpose. You may come to the realization that your previous job wasn’t aligned with your true desires or goals, prompting you to seek a path that is more fulfilling and authentic. This card is a nudge to trust that this job loss could be leading you toward a more purposeful and meaningful life.
Forgiveness and Letting Go
Judgment also signifies a time to release any guilt, regret, or grudges from the past. If you feel responsible for your job loss or harbor any negative feelings about past work experiences, this card advises you to let go. This cleansing of past baggage will help you move forward with clarity and peace of mind.

Suggestions and Actions

Take Time for Reflection
Use this moment to reflect on what led to the job loss and how you can grow from it. What lessons can you learn? What would you do differently next time? This introspection will help guide your next steps and ensure that your future decisions are aligned with your true desires.
Embrace Change and New Beginnings
The Judgment card indicates that now is the time for transformation. Embrace this opportunity to reinvent yourself or change career paths. If you’ve been considering a different field or starting something new, this is the perfect time to take action.
Make Bold Decisions
Judgment calls for decisive action. It’s not enough to just think about making changes—you need to make them. Update your resume, apply for new roles, consider additional training, or even start your own business. Trust in your abilities and take the necessary steps forward.
Release the Past
If you’re holding onto past mistakes, feelings of guilt, or anger related to the job loss, Judgment urges you to release these emotions. Forgiveness—of yourself and others—will help you make a clean break and move forward with clarity and a renewed sense of purpose.
Follow Your True Calling
Pay attention to any insights or realizations about your true passions and calling. If you’ve been feeling unfulfilled in your previous job, Judgment invites you to consider what truly excites and motivates you. This is an opportunity to align your career with your higher purpose.

Final Thought

Judgment upright signals that you’re at a crossroads, facing an opportunity for rebirth and transformation. While the job loss may feel like a setback, it’s actually a chance to reflect, learn, and make bold decisions that lead you toward a brighter, more fulfilling future. Embrace the change, let go of the past, and trust in your ability to rise to your true potential. The path forward is one of self-discovery, purpose, and new beginnings.
