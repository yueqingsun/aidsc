9. The Hermit
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Introspection and Reflection
The Hermit upright suggests that now is the time to pause and reflect on your life and career. Losing your job may have shaken you, but this card invites you to use this as an opportunity for self-discovery. Take some time to reflect on what you truly want from your career and what your values are. This could be a moment for deep personal growth and to reassess your goals.
Seeking Inner Wisdom
The Hermit represents the search for truth and understanding. It suggests that the answers to your questions about your next steps are not necessarily found outside of you but within. You may need to spend time alone, meditating or journaling, to uncover your deeper desires and find the path that resonates with your authentic self.
Solitude and Withdrawal
The Hermit often calls for solitude. It’s a time to withdraw from the noise of the world and focus on your inner life. You may need a period of solitude to recharge emotionally and mentally after the loss of your job. This time of withdrawal is not about isolation but about finding clarity through quiet reflection.
Wisdom from Experience
This card also speaks to the value of experience and wisdom. It encourages you to trust your own knowledge and insights gained from your past work experiences. You may already have the tools and wisdom needed to move forward, but you need to take a step back to recognize them.
Guidance and Mentorship
While The Hermit often focuses on inner wisdom, it can also suggest that you may seek guidance from a trusted mentor or advisor who has walked a similar path. This could be a time to connect with someone who can offer sage advice or help you see things from a different perspective.

Suggestions and Actions

Spend Time in Reflections
Take some time away from the job search to reflect deeply on your life and career. What are your core values? What do you truly want to contribute to the world through your work? Engage in practices like journaling, meditation, or simply sitting in quiet contemplation to gain clarity.
Reevaluate Your Career Goals
The Hermit invites you to reassess your goals. Losing a job can create space for you to think about what you really want in your career. Are there new directions you’d like to explore? Is this the time to pivot and try something completely different? Use this time of introspection to clarify your next steps.
Seek Mentorship or Guidance
While The Hermit encourages introspection, it also suggests seeking out a wise guide or mentor if needed. You don’t have to navigate this transition alone. Reach out to someone whose experience you admire and ask for their advice. Sometimes an external perspective can help you make sense of your internal reflections.
Embrace Solitude to Recharge
If you’re feeling emotionally drained from your job loss, The Hermit suggests taking time to recharge. Use this period to regain your mental clarity, heal emotionally, and restore your energy. Sometimes, the best way forward is to first replenish your reserves.
Trust Your Inner Wisdom
Trust that you already have the answers you need within yourself. Instead of seeking answers from external sources, look inward and listen to your intuition. You might already know what direction you want to take, but need quiet space to listen to that inner voice.

Final Thought

The Hermit upright encourages you to take a pause, reflect, and reconnect with your inner wisdom. It’s a reminder that sometimes the best way to move forward is by first taking a step back. By embracing solitude, seeking deeper insights, and trusting your own knowledge, you can find the clarity you need to make wise decisions in the wake of a job loss. This period of introspection will ultimately guide you toward the path that aligns with your true self.
