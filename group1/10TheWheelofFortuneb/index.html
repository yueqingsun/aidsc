10. The Wheel of Fortune
(Reversed)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Stagnation and Delay
The Wheel of Fortune reversed can indicate a feeling of stagnation or that things are not moving as quickly as you would like. After losing your job, you may be feeling stuck or frustrated by a lack of progress in your job search or personal life. You might feel like you're hitting a wall or that things are not working out in your favor.
Unforeseen Challenges
This card can signify that external circumstances or factors beyond your control are delaying progress or making it harder to move forward. It could be that you're facing obstacles that are preventing you from securing a new job or advancing in your career. However, these challenges may not be permanent, and you may need to adjust your approach or expectations to navigate through them.
Bad Luck or Timing Issues
The Wheel reversed often points to bad timing or events that feel unlucky. You may feel like your efforts aren't yielding results, and opportunities may not be presenting themselves when you need them. However, this card is a reminder that luck is cyclical and that these difficult times will pass—if you can keep moving forward.
Resistance to Change
Another interpretation of the reversed Wheel is resistance to change. After losing your job, you may be holding on too tightly to the past or to a particular expectation about what your career should look like. The reversed Wheel urges you to let go of old patterns or beliefs that are no longer serving you and be open to new possibilities.
Karmic Lessons
The Wheel of Fortune, even when reversed, can point to a time of karmic lessons. It might be that this situation—though difficult—holds valuable lessons for you. The challenges you are facing could be guiding you toward personal growth, teaching you how to handle adversity, or helping you re-align your path in the long term.

Suggestions and Actions

Reevaluate Your Approach
If you’re feeling stuck or that things aren’t moving in your favor, it’s time to reassess your current approach. Are there changes you need to make in how you’re looking for work or in how you’re handling this transition? Perhaps your search methods, mindset, or even the type of work you're seeking need some adjustment to align with the changing circumstances.
Embrace Flexibility
The Wheel reversed suggests a need for flexibility. If your job search or career path isn’t progressing as expected, try to shift your perspective. You may need to adapt to new opportunities, change industries, or learn new skills. The resistance to change could be blocking progress, so try to remain open-minded and embrace new opportunities when they arise.
Focus on the Present Moment
Rather than focusing too much on the future or worrying about the lack of movement, The Wheel of Fortune reversed encourages you to focus on the present moment. Sometimes things take time to fall into place. By grounding yourself in the here and now, you may be able to find small, meaningful steps that can lead to bigger shifts.
Let Go of Control
The reversed Wheel often signals a need to release control and trust that things will eventually move in your favor. There may be factors outside of your control, but this doesn’t mean you’re powerless. Accept that there are cycles of good and bad luck, and while this may be a down phase, the cycle will eventually turn in your favor again.
Look for Lessons in Setbacks
Although this situation may feel unlucky, The Wheel reversed can offer you an opportunity to learn and grow from these setbacks. Take a step back and reflect on what this period of job loss is teaching you. Are there patterns in your life or career that you need to address? How can you use this time to improve yourself or your situation moving forward?

Final Thought

The Wheel of Fortune reversed suggests that you may be in a period where things feel out of alignment, but this phase is temporary. While you may be facing obstacles or delays, the cycle of fortune is always shifting, and things will eventually turn in your favor. By embracing flexibility, adjusting your approach, and staying patient, you can navigate through this period and come out stronger on the other side. This card reminds you that even in times of bad luck, change is inevitable, and fortune can turn at any moment.
