7. The Chariot
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Determination and Willpower
The Chariot represents inner strength, determination, and the ability to overcome challenges. In the context of job loss, it suggests that now is the time to take charge of your career and actively pursue new opportunities. The card encourages you to stay focused on your goals and use your personal drive to overcome any setbacks.
Focused Action and Direction
The Chariot asks you to set clear, specific goals and pursue them with determination. You may feel uncertain or overwhelmed after losing your job, but the card urges you to take proactive steps toward your next career move. It’s not a time to be passive or indecisive; instead, you should confidently move forward with a sense of direction.
Overcoming Obstacles
The Chariot is often about facing challenges head-on. It suggests that although you may encounter obstacles on your path, you have the strength and resilience to overcome them. Whether it’s tough competition, a lack of opportunity, or self-doubt, the card signals that you have the tools to succeed.
Control and Balance
The Chariot also speaks to the need for balance and control. It’s about managing your emotions and staying focused on your ultimate goals, even when faced with distractions. You may need to juggle multiple tasks or responsibilities, but with discipline and focus, you can steer yourself in the right direction.
Self-Belief and Confidence
Confidence is a central theme of The Chariot. It encourages you to believe in your abilities and trust that you can achieve your goals. This card often comes up when you need to tap into your self-belief to move forward successfully.

Suggestions and Actions

Set Clear, Specific Goals
After losing your job, it's important to define what you want to achieve next. Take time to set clear, achievable career goals. This could be updating your resume, applying for specific jobs, or learning new skills. Be specific and strategic about what you want to accomplish, and take the first step toward those goals.
Take Decisive Action
The Chariot urges you to act decisively. Don’t wait for opportunities to come to you—go after them. This could mean reaching out to potential employers, networking aggressively, or exploring different industries. By taking control and making bold moves, you increase your chances of success.
Maintain Focus and Discipline
Stay disciplined in your efforts. Set a daily or weekly schedule for job applications, networking, and skill development. Keeping focused on your goals, rather than becoming distracted or discouraged by setbacks, will help you make steady progress.
Stay Positive and Confident
Even though you may be facing challenges, The Chariot reminds you to stay confident in your ability to succeed. Trust that you can navigate obstacles and that each step you take brings you closer to your desired outcome.
Embrace Challenges as Opportunities
Rather than seeing challenges as setbacks, view them as opportunities for growth and learning. The Chariot represents overcoming adversity, so take any difficulties as a chance to prove your resilience and strength.

Final Thought

The Chariot upright is a card of action, control, and triumph over adversity. It invites you to take charge of your situation, set clear goals, and push forward with determination. By maintaining focus, discipline, and confidence, you can navigate this transition and come out victorious on the other side. Keep your eyes on the prize and stay persistent—the road ahead is yours to conquer.
