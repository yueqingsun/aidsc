11. Justice
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Fairness and Accountability
Justice asks you to reflect on the fairness of the situation. Losing your job may have been outside of your control, but it’s important to examine how your actions, decisions, or circumstances may have contributed to this situation. This is a time for self-reflection and acknowledging any role you may have played in the current outcome. By taking responsibility for your part, you can gain clarity on what to do next.
Decision Making
The card indicates that now is a time to make clear, balanced decisions. You may need to weigh your options carefully and consider the consequences of your choices. Justice encourages you to think logically and avoid making decisions based on emotion or impulse. You might need to create a fair, rational plan for your next steps, whether it involves finding a new job, shifting careers, or making lifestyle changes.
Karma and Consequences
Justice is often associated with karma—what you put out into the world comes back to you. This card suggests that the situation you’re in may be a result of karmic forces, either from past actions or from the universe’s natural balancing process. While this may feel difficult, know that if you’ve been fair and responsible, good outcomes may come in the future. If there are areas where you need to correct past mistakes or injustices, now is the time to address them.
Truth and Clarity
Justice invites you to seek the truth and make decisions based on facts, not assumptions or emotions. If you’ve lost your job, take time to review the details—was it a decision based on your performance, company restructuring, or other factors? Understanding the full picture will help you make informed choices about your next steps.
Balance and Integrity
The Justice card asks you to maintain balance and integrity in how you move forward. In times of uncertainty, it’s easy to feel like taking shortcuts or acting unfairly to get ahead. However, Justice encourages you to stay true to your moral compass, ensuring that any actions you take align with your values and long-term goals. Maintaining integrity will set you up for success in the future.

Suggestions and Actions

Evaluate Your Past Actions
Reflect on the decisions or actions that led to your job loss. Have there been any areas where you could have made different choices? Use this time to learn from the past, acknowledging mistakes if necessary, and take responsibility for your role in the situation. This self-awareness will help you move forward with greater clarity and integrity.
Make Objective, Balanced Decisions
In this period of uncertainty, it’s important to make decisions with a calm, clear mind. Weigh all your options before acting, whether you’re deciding to apply for new jobs, pursue a career change, or further your education. Avoid rushing into decisions without considering the full impact of your choices. Justice encourages thoughtful, balanced action.
Seek Clarity and Transparency
If you're unsure about the reasons behind your job loss or feel there was an injustice, seek clarity. If it’s appropriate, ask for feedback or clarification from your former employer or HR department. Understanding the facts surrounding your job loss can help you feel more empowered as you move forward.
Align Your Actions with Your Values
Moving forward, ensure that your decisions are in alignment with your core values and what feels just and fair to you. If your former job did not align with your true purpose or values, this is an opportunity to find a role that better matches who you are and what you stand for. Make integrity a cornerstone of your next move.
Trust in the Law of Karma
If you feel like the situation is unfair, remember that the universe has a way of balancing things out. If this is a setback, know that your efforts will eventually be recognized, and the truth will come to light. Trust that you will find a way to rectify any past missteps and that future opportunities will reflect the energy you put into the world.

Final Thought

Justice upright encourages you to take responsibility, seek clarity, and act with integrity in the face of adversity. Losing your job is a challenging situation, but it’s also an opportunity to make fair, balanced decisions and align your future with your values. By trusting the process, embracing fairness, and understanding the consequences of your actions, you can navigate this period with wisdom and emerge stronger. Stay grounded, honest, and fair with yourself as you take the next steps forward.
