13. Death
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Endings and Closure
Losing your job represents a significant ending, and the upright Death card acknowledges this closure. It encourages you to accept this as a necessary step in your journey, even if it feels difficult right now. Holding onto the past will only hinder your growth.
Transformation and Growth
This card signals that major changes are underway, and you are being invited to transform your perspective, habits, and approach. Use this moment to reflect on how this loss can be an opportunity for personal and professional growth.
Letting Go of the Old
To move forward, you must release what is no longer serving you—whether that’s old career aspirations, limiting beliefs, or attachments to the past. This shedding process can feel uncomfortable but is essential for new opportunities to emerge.
A Doorway to New Opportunities
The Hanged Man also symbolizes spiritual growth and the power of patience. This is a time to connect with your inner self, reflect on your values, and align with your deeper purpose. Trust that this period of suspension is temporary and that it’s paving the way for future progress.
Catalyst for Renewal
Losing your job might be the wake-up call you needed to realign your career and life goals. This card asks you to evaluate what you truly want and use this time of transition as a chance to start fresh.

Suggestions and Actions

Accept the End
Acknowledge and accept that your previous job or career chapter has come to an end. Allow yourself to grieve the loss if needed, but don’t dwell on it. Acceptance is the first step toward moving forward.
Reflect on What to Let Go
Consider what habits, attitudes, or beliefs need to be released. For example, if you’ve been holding onto a rigid definition of success, now is the time to let it go and explore new ways to define fulfillment.
Embrace Change
Change can feel intimidating, but it’s also a chance to grow. Look at this moment as an opportunity to reinvent yourself or pursue paths you may have previously dismissed. Be open to new ideas and directions.
Set New Goals
Use this transitional period to reflect on what truly matters to you. What kind of work brings you joy? What kind of environment supports your growth? Set goals that align with your values and vision for the future.
Prepare for the Rebirth
Recognize that this ending is paving the way for a fresh start. Whether it’s learning a new skill, exploring a different industry, or starting your own business, take small steps to prepare for the opportunities that lie ahead.

Final Thought

The upright Death card invites you to see your job loss not as an end, but as a profound moment of transformation. While letting go of the past can be challenging, it is the first step toward renewal and growth. Trust that this transition is clearing the way for something better and that by embracing change, you’ll find yourself on a more fulfilling and aligned path.
