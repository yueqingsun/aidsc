10. The Wheel of Fortune
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Cycles and Change
The Wheel of Fortune upright represents the cycles of life. Losing your job may feel like a setback, but it could also be a natural part of a larger cycle in your life. This card indicates that changes are happening, and you may soon find yourself in a new phase. It suggests that this job loss, though difficult, is part of a bigger picture that will eventually lead to a better situation.
Fortune and Opportunity
This card signals that luck and good fortune are on the horizon. The Wheel of Fortune often brings unexpected opportunities or positive turns in circumstances. You might soon encounter a new job or opportunity that aligns more closely with your goals or values, though it might come in an unexpected form. The key is to stay open and adaptable to the changes that come your way.
Destiny and Fate
The Wheel of Fortune indicates that your situation is influenced by forces beyond your control. While you can take actions to improve your situation, the card suggests that some elements of this transition are fated. This could be an important time of personal growth or realignment with your true path, even if the process feels challenging at first.
Timing
The Wheel also speaks to the importance of timing. The right opportunity will present itself at the right time, and it may require patience as the cycle plays out. Trust that things are unfolding as they should, even if you can’t see the full picture yet. This card invites you to trust in the flow of events and allow things to unfold naturally.
Change of Luck
If you’ve been feeling stuck or unlucky in your job search, The Wheel of Fortune upright indicates a shift. This could be the turning point where things start to improve. Whether it's finding a new opportunity, receiving an unexpected offer, or experiencing a sudden change in circumstances, fortune is turning in your favor.

Suggestions and Actions

Embrace Change
The Wheel of Fortune urges you to be open to the changes that are coming. Instead of resisting the transition or fearing the unknown, embrace the idea that this moment of uncertainty could lead to something better. Stay flexible and adaptable, as the next opportunity may present itself in a way you don’t expect.
Trust the Timing
Be patient during this time. The Wheel reminds you that everything has its proper time. Trust that the right opportunity will come when the time is right. While it may not be immediate, things are moving behind the scenes, and change is on the way.
Take Advantage of Opportunities
When opportunities arise, even if they seem unexpected or different from what you originally had in mind, take advantage of them. The Wheel of Fortune often brings sudden shifts that can change the course of your life for the better. Don’t hesitate to take chances and explore new possibilities.
Reflect on Patterns
The Wheel of Fortune suggests that this situation may be part of a repeating cycle or pattern in your life. Reflect on previous transitions or job changes you've experienced and how they've shaped you. This can give you insight into how you can approach this period of change with more confidence and awareness.
Stay Positive and Open-Minded
Focus on staying positive and open to what the future holds. Even if your current situation feels uncertain, trust that a new opportunity will arise. Stay optimistic, as the Wheel of Fortune often brings rewards to those who keep faith and are willing to ride the waves of change.

Final Thought

The Wheel of Fortune upright signifies that this period of uncertainty is temporary and that things are about to shift in your favor. Embrace the changes that come with the loss of your job and trust that fortune is on your side. By remaining flexible, optimistic, and open to opportunities, you will find that this transition leads to greater growth and fulfillment. The wheel is turning in your favor—let it guide you toward a brighter future.
