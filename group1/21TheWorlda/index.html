21. The World
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Completion and Achievementn
The World signifies the successful completion of a cycle or chapter. Losing your job may feel like an ending, but this card suggests that you have reached a point where you have gained valuable experience and are now prepared for a new and rewarding chapter. You may have learned key lessons that will help you in your next endeavor.
Wholeness and Balance
The World represents a sense of balance and harmony, which suggests that after the turmoil of losing a job, you are moving toward a time of greater stability. You may find that you feel more aligned with your true self and your goals, ready to manifest your desires in a more balanced way.
New Opportunities and Expansive Growth
This card indicates that the end of your current job is not the end of your journey, but rather the beginning of something new. The World often points to opportunities that are expansive, meaning that new ventures, projects, or career paths will allow you to grow in ways that you may not have anticipated.
Global Perspective and Broader Horizons
The World card often comes up when you are ready to expand your horizons, either through travel, new experiences, or by engaging with a wider network or audience. It could be a signal that your next job or venture might be something broader or more global, pushing you outside of your comfort zone but bringing greater rewards.
A Reward for Your Efforts
The World is a card of success, recognition, and accomplishment. It suggests that you have the skills and talents to succeed in whatever path you choose next. Your previous efforts have set you up for success, and this is a time to reap the rewards of all the work you’ve done.

Suggestions and Actions

Embrace New Beginnings
The World encourages you to embrace this ending as a stepping stone to something better. Rather than fearing the future, look forward to the fresh opportunities ahead. Use this time to set new goals and take proactive steps toward realizing them, whether that’s finding a new job, starting a business, or exploring different career paths.
Reflect on Your Journey
Take a moment to reflect on all that you’ve learned and accomplished. Consider how your previous experiences, skills, and strengths can guide you in your next role or chapter. The World suggests that you are ready for the next level of achievement, so it’s important to acknowledge your growth so far.
Stay Open to Global Opportunities
Be open to opportunities that allow you to expand your reach, whether that’s by exploring international roles, freelance work, or something that aligns with global trends. The World may signal that it’s time to think bigger and explore new career possibilities outside of your usual boundaries.
Celebrate Your Accomplishments
If you’re feeling discouraged after losing your job, The World encourages you to recognize how far you’ve come. You’ve likely achieved much in your career already, and now it’s time to celebrate that success. Take pride in your abilities and the achievements that have led you here, and use that confidence as fuel for your next move.
Plan for the Future
With the energy of The World supporting you, this is a great time to start planning for your next steps. Look ahead at your career path and consider how you can make the most of your experience and talents. Whether it’s applying for new roles, starting a creative project, or further developing your skills, The World invites you to step confidently into your future.

Final Thought

The World upright signals the end of one cycle and the beginning of another, full of growth and potential. This card tells you that you are ready for something bigger and more fulfilling, and that your previous efforts have prepared you for this next chapter. Embrace this opportunity with confidence, knowing that the world is wide open, and success is yours to claim.
