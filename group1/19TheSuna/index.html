19. The Sun
(Upright)

Card Meaning and Analysis

Clarity and Confidence
The Sun upright suggests that clarity will soon emerge, helping you see your situation in a new light. It’s a reminder that you have the energy, confidence, and resources to move forward. You'll soon be able to clearly see the next steps in your career path, with a sense of confidence and optimism.
Success and Achievement
This card is a sign of success on the horizon. Even though you’ve lost your job, The Sun assures you that you will achieve your goals in the near future. The period of uncertainty is about to end, and you will soon experience a sense of accomplishment or find a new position that is even more rewarding.
Positive Energy and Renewal
The Sun represents a fresh start or a burst of positive energy. If you’ve been feeling down about your job loss, this card suggests that you will regain your enthusiasm and motivation. It’s a time to embrace new opportunities with a renewed sense of purpose and vitality.
Joy and Abundance
The Sun brings abundance in many forms—financial, emotional, and spiritual. It suggests that the loss of your job could open the door to greater opportunities, and you may even find that you enjoy a sense of freedom or personal growth as you move forward.
Enlightenment and Self-Expression
This card often represents self-expression and creative energy. It may be a time to explore new areas of interest, perhaps something you’ve always wanted to do but never had the time for. The Sun encourages you to pursue your passions and find joy in the process of reinventing your career.

Suggestions and Actions

Focus on Positive Thinking
The Sun urges you to maintain an optimistic mindset. While losing a job can feel discouraging, focusing on the positive aspects of your situation—such as the possibility of exploring new opportunities—can help you move forward with confidence.
Take Action with Confidence
With the clarity and energy The Sun brings, take proactive steps toward your next goal. Update your resume, apply for new jobs, or even consider starting your own business or project. Trust that your efforts will be met with success.
Celebrate Your Strengths
Take this time to recognize your strengths and talents. The Sun encourages self-confidence and self-expression. Reflect on what you’re truly good at and how you can incorporate those skills into your next career move.
Stay Open to New Opportunities
This card suggests that new opportunities are coming your way, so stay open to different possibilities. Don’t limit yourself to the same type of work you’ve done before—explore new career paths, freelance opportunities, or even creative endeavors.
Nurture Your Well-being
The Sun is a reminder of the importance of self-care. Take care of your physical and emotional health during this time of transition. Stay active, connect with loved ones, and engage in activities that bring you joy and renewal.

Final Thought

The Sun upright is one of the most positive cards in the tarot deck, bringing messages of success, clarity, and new beginnings. Despite the challenges of losing your job, this card reminds you that brighter days are ahead. It’s a time to embrace your confidence, stay optimistic, and take positive action toward your future. New opportunities are on the horizon, and you’re more than capable of achieving the success and happiness you desire.
